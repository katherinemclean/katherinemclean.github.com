ABOUT ME

I am from McLean, VA just outside Washington, D.C. Currently I reside in Boston, MA and recently graduated from the School of the Museum of Fine Arts. I specialize in drawing, printmaking, and papermaking. Click on the Resume link above to learn more.

ARTIST'S STATEMENT

Using drawing, inking, printing and papermaking I comment on mental and emotional states. I do this by playing with scenarios of social interactions, different levels of masking and demasking and how they intertwine. doing this allows a dialogue to begin between the viewer and the work.

The ideas of self manipulation of emotions is less of an idea and more of a reality. A part of my work is making an exaggerated emotional mirror covered with dark humor and creepy fun, allowing the viewer to be apart of the piece while looking on the outside.
