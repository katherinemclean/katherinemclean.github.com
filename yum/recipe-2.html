Moscato Cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 c All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 t Baking Soda
  • 3/4 t Salt
  • 1 1/4 c Sugar
  • 1 c Canola Oil
  • 1/3 c Moscato Wine
  • 3/4 c Buttermilk
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 t White Vinegar
  • Optional: Berries or sprinkles for garnish

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, salt, and sugar, and whisk together.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the oil, Moscato, buttermilk, eggs, and vinegar.
  4. Slowly pour the wet ingredients into dry and mix until it is just blended.
  5. Bake in paper cupcake cups for 15-17 minutes.

Yield: approximately 12 to 18 cupcakes
